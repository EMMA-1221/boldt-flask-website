Welcome to the Team Development Tool!

The team evaluator was developed in order to form teams that had the highest probability of project success.

There are two methods for team development strategies:
  • Team Building Guidance
  • Team Performance Estimator

Contract Type — Impact on Profit Margin

Contract TypeModelMetricImpact
Lump Sum (LS)Full DatasetFPMIncreases (+0.014)
Lump Sum (LS)Full DatasetCIMIncreases (+0.021)
Time & Materials (TM)Full DatasetFPMIncreases (+0.052)
Integrated Project DeliveryFull DatasetFPMDecreases (-0.12)

Projects that utilize Lump Sum contracts will lead to slightly higher profit margins while projects utilizing Time and Materials contracts typically lead to significantly higher profit margins. Projects that use Integrated Project Delivery contracts typically lead to lower profit margins with the possibility for negative margins.

Recommendation: Projects with Lump Sum or Time and Materials contracts should be prioritized for maximizing profit margins.

For shorter projects, duration has a significant impact on profit margins. Projects under 101 days tend to underperform, while those in the 101–146.5 day range typically yield the highest margins. Lump Sum and Time & Materials contracts perform well in this range.

Recommendation: Aim for project durations between 101 and 146.5 days to maximize profit margins on shorter projects.

Recommendation: For long-duration projects, prioritize Lump Sum contracts. Avoid Time & Materials contracts for projects exceeding one year.

Understanding the Success Estimate

The project team is only a component of what brings project success. Because of this, the estimates in the table are moderately accurate.

It is suggested to look at the 'Results' row and try to change team members until you get a '3rd quartile' or 'Top 1/4' result.

From testing, the tool correctly guesses an above average team 64% of the time.

The $10M+ model relies on elements from the Project Manager, Project Executive, and the team as a whole.
