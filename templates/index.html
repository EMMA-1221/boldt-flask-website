Welcome to the Team Development Tool!

The team evaluator was developed in order to form teams that had the highest probability of project success.

There are two methods for team development strategies:
  • Team Building Guidance
  • Team Performance Estimator

Recommendation: Projects in the Power and Industrial sectors should be prioritized for maximizing profit margins. As the project industry shifts to Healthcare or Institutional, profit margins and change in margin decrease drastically.

Understanding the Success Estimate

The Success Estimate is generated using regression tree models. The tool predicts two metrics: Final Profit Margin (FPM) and Change in Margin (CIM).

The project team is only one of many factors that influence project outcomes. As a result, predictions should be used as a general guide rather than a definitive forecast.

It is recommended to use the Results row as a reference and experiment with different team compositions to find combinations that yield stronger predicted outcomes.
